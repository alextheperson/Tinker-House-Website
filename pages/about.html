What is Tinker House?

Our mission

Our mission is to help people (short and tall) learn about circitry and programing(I programed this website with a friend named Zach). I offer kits to help people learn about electonics, and hopefully there will be some more stuff comming soon.

Our Story

Tinker House(formerly named Circuit Circus), was started because of a craft fair. A long time ago I(Alex) was going to be participating in a craft fair. I knew I wanted to sell circuitry kits, because I thought 'why aren't there any electronics kits made by kids?' and that is why I stared Circuit Circus. The craft fair went very poorly but it also went kind of well. I recently found out that Circuit Circus is the name of a inderactive design studio, so I changed the name to Tinker House. I also thought 'Tinker House' fit a little better. Later a friend named Zach asked if he could be a part of Tinker House and I agreed. He then went on to help with a conact response sorting program, and helped build this website.

That Is Our Story.
